# Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We commit to acting in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, and healthy community.

Treat everyone with respect. Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully, take responsibility for mistakes, and focus on what is best for the community. Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, personal attacks, publishing others' private information, and any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

Violations may be reported to the maintainer. Depending on severity, consequences range from a private warning up to a permanent ban from the community.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
